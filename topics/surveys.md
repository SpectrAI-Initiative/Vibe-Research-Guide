<h1 align="center">Vibe Research Guide<br>Surveys</h1>

> Survey and perspective papers are still the fastest way to build a clean mental model of Vibe Research. The key is to read them in the right order: first the field-level map, then the scientific-method framing, then the system and benchmark view.

## How To Read This Page

There are now three different kinds of overview papers in this space:

1. **Field maps**: what the whole area looks like.
2. **Scientific-method framing**: how LLMs map onto hypothesis, experimentation, writing, and review.
3. **Agent-architecture surveys**: how memory, planning, tool use, and multi-agent structure work underneath.

If you mix those categories together, the field feels blurry. If you separate them, the landscape becomes much easier to navigate.

## Reading Tips

1. Start with **From Automation to Autonomy** if you want one paper that explains why this field now matters.
2. Read **LLM Agents as AI Scientists** next to get the full workflow view.
3. Read **Exploring the role of large language models in the scientific method** when you want a more rigorous research lens than generic agent discourse.
4. Read **A Survey of LLM-based Scientific Agents** once you are ready to compare systems and benchmarks.
5. Keep the two architecture surveys for later unless you are actively building.
